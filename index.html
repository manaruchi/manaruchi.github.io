SPI Utility for QGIS

The Standardized Precipitation Index (SPI), a drought index widely used for drought monitoring, is estimated based on the probability of deficit in precipitation at any time scale. It is a simple and flexible drought index as it requires only precipitation as input data and it can quantify the degree of dryness/wetness on multiple time scales to address different types of droughts.
